*Fighting Style Feat (Prerequisite: Fighting Style Feature)*

You gain the following benefits.
- ***Thrown Weapon Damage.*** When you hit with a ranged attack roll using a weapon that has the Thrown property, you gain a +2 bonus to the damage roll.
