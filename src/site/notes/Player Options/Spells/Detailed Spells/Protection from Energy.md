# Protection from Energy

_Level 1 Abjuration ([[Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])_
**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

For the duration the willing creature you touch has Resistance to one damage type of your choice: Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
