# Darkvision

_Level 2 Transmutation ([[Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])_
**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (a dried carrot)  
**Duration:** 8 hours

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has Darkvision with a range of 150 feet.
