# Stoneskin

_Level 4 Transmutation ([[Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])_
**Casting Time:** Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100+ GP, which the spell consumes)  
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch has Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
