# Lesser Restoration

_Level 2 Abjuration ([[Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]], [[Player Options/Classes/Bard/Bard\|Bard]], [[Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]])_
**Casting Time:** Bonus Action  
**Range:** Touch  
**Components:** V, S  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

You touch a creature and end one condition on it: [[Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Blinded\|Blinded]], [[Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Deafened\|Deafened]], [[Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Paralyzed\|Paralyzed]], or [[Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Poisoned\|Poisoned]].
